Sourcemod plugins

A collection of sourcemod plugins I've made or helped make for Team Fortress 2.
Centered around the jumping community and Tempus Network.

sm-translate

A TF2 plugin for real-time chat translation using Google Cloud Translation API.

Source: https://github.com/laurirasanen/sm-translate/

Groundfix

A plugin developed by jayess and I.
Made with the idea of preventing rampbugs, which occasionally make you stop suddenly when sliding along a ramp, but further expanded to cover other issues as well.

Source: https://github.com/laurirasanen/groundfix/

Stopstickybounce

A plugin intended for stopping stickybombs from bouncing off other players.
Even though stickybombs should not bounce off your teammates, occasionally they still do. This usually happens if you hit them in the head. This little plugin makes stickies pass straight through teammates and even enemies if you so wish. Intended for the jumping community, because it's annoying to have your sticky fly to God knows where when there are multiple people on the same part of the map.

Made in collaboration with jayess and JoinedSenses.
https://github.com/jsza/
https://github.com/joinedsenses

Source: https://github.com/laurirasanen/stopstickybounce/
A more up-to-date version can be found at https://github.com/JoinedSenses/TF2-Collision-Projectiles
