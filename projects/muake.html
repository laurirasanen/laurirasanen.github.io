Postmortem

Majority of gameplay programming as well as the task of networking fell on to me because I had some previous experience from Unity multiplayer games.
In hindsight the amount of code that required networking in one form or another was way too much for me to handle on my own.
Due to this some features, such as matchmaking, had to be cut as the deadline approached.
Overall this project was a great learning experience about Unity networking and about the importance of division of labor.
