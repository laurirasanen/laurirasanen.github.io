MushroomJam

A simple 2D platformer that I made in 48 hours for Kajaani University of Applied Science Back to School Game Jam 2017.
The jam theme was 'Mushroom', and the first thing that popped into my head was a reverse type Mario game, where you play as a mushroom and goomba stomp humans.
Includes 3 levels and a built in timer for speedrunning.
The game itself was actually made in the first 24 hours of the jam, after which I slept for most of the remaining 24 hours.

Source and download: https://github.com/laurirasanen/mushroomjam
