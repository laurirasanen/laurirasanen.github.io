Writer Ride

A co-op game made in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2018/FGJX, where the theme was 'Transmission'.
Your goal is to make a car go fast by typing while avoiding obstacles.
One player controls the keyboard, and another controls the mouse.

Postmortem

For me this project was a bit of a disaster.
Most of the things I made had to either be scrapped or remade completely due to us not having a clear design in mind when we began work.
Only things that ended up in the final product from me were some UI code, obstacle dragging, and enemy controller.
